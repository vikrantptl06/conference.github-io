About Conference

The conference aims to provide a platform for academic scholars, industry professionals, and research students to discuss contemporary issues and advancements in the field of global business. This year’s theme focuses on the intersection of technology, sustainability, and innovation in a borderless world. The conference will feature thought-provoking keynote speeches, engaging panel discussions, and presentations of original research.

Participants will gain insights into emerging trends and challenges, explore practical solutions, and network with experts and peers from around the globe. The event encourages interdisciplinary collaboration and knowledge sharing, with the ultimate goal of shaping the future of global business practices.

Paper Submission Guidelines

PAPER SUBMISSION GUIDELINES

The participants may submit an Extended Abstract (not exceeding 750 words) or a Full Paper (not exceeding 7000 words, including tables, figures, and references) in the following format:

  • Purpose/Objectives
  • Literature Review
  • Study Design/Methodology/Approach
  • Data Analysis & Findings
  • Originality
  • Implications of the Study
  • Research Limitations & Conclusion
  • References

Format Guidelines:

  • Abstracts & full papers should use 14-point font size for headings, 12-point font size for body text, and Times New Roman font in MS Word with 1.5 line spacing.
  • References must follow APA format.
  • The first page of the manuscript should include the title of the paper, name of the author(s), affiliating institute, contact number, and email address.

PAPER PUBLICATION OPPORTUNITIES

Selected research papers may be considered for publication as per the peer review process of the following journals:

  • Foreign Trade Review (FTR), Sage
  • Journal of Global Marketing, Taylor & Francis
  • FIIB Business Review, Sage
  • The Learning Organization, Emerald
  • Review of Management Literature, Emerald
  • Other leading journals (under discussion)
  • Chapters in edited books may also be considered
